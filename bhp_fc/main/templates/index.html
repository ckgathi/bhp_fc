About

The Origin of the team

The team has originally been formed by the BHP male members for team building within work, keep fit and for fun. Through out the years the team stopped functioning.

The team has recently been brought back by the team captain and with the help of other team members. The team still has the same purpose to keep fit, team building within the organization and for fun. So far the team has achieved a lot and people even know each other that would have otherwise not known each other if it was not for the team.
